SmartGrama provides welfare and microfinance services for citizens.

A welfare loan helps low-income citizens during financial difficulties.

Loan eligibility depends on income, expenses, assets, repayment history, and existing loans.

If monthly expenses are high, the system may recommend a smaller loan amount.

A guarantor may be required for higher-risk applicants.

The SmartGrama wallet shows active loans, welfare payments, installment history, and payment dates.

Users can check their application status from the dashboard.

Welfare eligibility depends on family size, income, dependents, and asset ownership.

Blockchain logs are used for secure verification and audit tracking.

The AI prediction system estimates repayment ability and loan risk level.
