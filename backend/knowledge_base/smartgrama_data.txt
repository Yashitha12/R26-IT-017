PROFILE INFORMATION:
A user can create a SmartGrama profile by entering personal details, address, GN division, employment details, income information, family details, and existing financial information.

LOAN INFORMATION:
A welfare loan helps low-income citizens during financial difficulties. Loan eligibility depends on income, monthly expenses, assets, repayment history, existing loans, and financial stability. If monthly expenses are high, the system may recommend a smaller loan amount. Higher-risk applicants may need a guarantor before loan approval.

WALLET INFORMATION:
The SmartGrama wallet shows active loans, remaining balances, installment history, welfare payments, payment dates, and transaction records.

WELFARE INFORMATION:
Welfare eligibility depends on family size, income level, dependents, asset ownership, and Grama Niladhari verification. Welfare programs include Samurdhi welfare support, senior citizen allowances, and disability assistance programs.

AI PREDICTION INFORMATION:
The AI prediction system estimates repayment ability, loan affordability, repayment duration, and loan risk level. Risk level represents the probability of repayment difficulties or loan default.

DASHBOARD INFORMATION:
The SmartGrama dashboard allows users to apply for loans, apply for welfare, access their wallet, view profile details, check application status, and communicate with the AI assistant.

BLOCKCHAIN INFORMATION:
Blockchain logs are used for secure verification, tamper-proof record keeping, and audit tracking.

CHATBOT INFORMATION:
The SmartGrama AI assistant supports Sinhala and English languages using Retrieval-Augmented Generation, translation services, and speech recognition.
