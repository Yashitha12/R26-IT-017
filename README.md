# SmartGrama – AI-Driven Microfinance and Welfare Management Platform

## Introduction

Traditional microfinance and welfare management systems in Sri Lanka are still heavily dependent on manual paperwork, isolated databases, and human decision-making processes. These limitations create delays, reduce transparency, increase the possibility of fraud, and make accurate loan and welfare eligibility evaluation difficult in rural communities.

To address these issues, SmartGrama proposes an integrated digital platform that combines Artificial Intelligence (AI), Blockchain Technology, Smart Contracts, and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to modernize rural financial and welfare services at Grama Niladhari (GN) division level.

---

# Project Overview

SmartGrama is a research-based digital platform designed to support microfinance institutions, welfare authorities, GN officers, and rural citizens through an intelligent and transparent management system.

The system provides:
- AI-based microfinance risk prediction
- Personalized loan recommendation support
- Welfare eligibility verification
- Blockchain-based data validation
- Smart contract automation
- Multilingual communication support

The platform aims to improve decision-making efficiency, reduce manual processing, and provide secure and transparent service management for underserved rural communities in Sri Lanka.

# Research Contribution

This research contributes to the field of rural financial technology by introducing an integrated intelligent decision-support platform specifically designed for low-resource rural environments.

The proposed system contributes through:
- AI-based personalized microfinance decision support
- Hybrid blockchain architecture for secure verification
- Smart contract-based eligibility automation
- Multilingual citizen interaction support

---

# Research Context

Many rural financial institutions and welfare systems in Sri Lanka still rely on manual operations and fragmented record-keeping methods. Existing digital loan prediction systems are mostly designed for formal banking sectors and require highly structured financial data.

However, rural borrowers often have:
- Irregular income patterns
- Limited formal financial records
- Alternative financial behaviors
- Limited digital accessibility

Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent and accessible platform that can support both financial institutions and rural citizens using alternative socio-economic data and automated decision-support mechanisms.

SmartGrama addresses this research gap by combining AI, Blockchain, Smart Contracts, and NLP technologies into a single integrated platform.
